# RELATIONSHIP SCHEMA

Relationship records document important people and the user's relationship with them over time.

They should be factual, careful, and fair to uncertainty.

---

# Relationship Overview

Short factual summary of who this person is in the user's life.

Do not overstate closeness or conflict without evidence.

---

- First Known Contact:

- Periods of Distance:
- Current Status:

---

# Key Incidents

Link to incidents involving this relationship.

For each incident, record:
- date or range
- certainty
- role of the person
- consequence for the relationship

---

# Emotional Significance

Document what the relationship meant to the user, based only on source material.

Separate:
- what happened
- how the user felt
- what the user believes now
- what remains uncertain

---

# Contradictions or Sensitive Issues

Record conflicting memories, unresolved interpretations, and sensitive claims carefully.

Do not assign motive unless the user explicitly states it or reliable evidence supports it.

---

# Open Questions

List targeted questions that could clarify chronology, significance, or unresolved events.
