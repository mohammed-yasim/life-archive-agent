# Digital Life Archive

Store records about devices, accounts, online platforms, usernames, online communities, files, chats, games, apps, and digital habits.

Use `DIGITAL_LIFE_SCHEMA.md` and `templates/digital-life-template.md`.

Digital-life records can help reconstruct chronology through:
- device ownership
- account creation dates
- message histories
- screenshots
- email accounts
- cloud backups
- platform changes
- online friendships

Treat private digital material as sensitive source evidence.
