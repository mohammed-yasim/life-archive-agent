# Archive

This directory contains durable life records.

The archive is for long-term preservation. Working theories, unresolved questions, and temporary investigations belong in `../working-memory/` until they are ready to become durable records.

---

# Subdirectories

- `timeline/` - chronological life records
- `incidents/` - significant events and episodes
- `eras/` - life phase records
- `people/` - person and relationship-adjacent records
- `places/` - homes, schools, neighborhoods, cities, workplaces, and recurring locations
- `digital-life/` - devices, accounts, platforms, usernames, online communities, and digital records
- `journals/` - diary-like material or dated reflective writing
- `autobiography/` - chapter drafts and narrative nonfiction
- `raw-notes/` - source memory and minimally processed notes

---

# Archive Rule

Do not allow polished writing to replace source material.
